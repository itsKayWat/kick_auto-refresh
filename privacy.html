Privacy Policy for Kick Auto Refresh Plus

Effective Date: 02/12/2024

Introduction

Kick Auto Refresh Plus is a browser extension designed to automatically refresh Kick.com livestreams when they encounter connection issues, bandwidth problems, or bot checks.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. It operates entirely on the user's device and does not transmit any data to external servers.

Data Usage

Since no data is collected, there is no data usage to disclose.

Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties.

User Rights

As no data is collected, users have no data-related rights to exercise.

Contact Information
